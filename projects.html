Lower Limb Exoskeleton Research Proposal

Researching the study design, equipment, and control data needed for a lower limb exoskeleton focused on assisting hip, knee, and ankle motion. The proposal covers motion capture, pressure pads, IMUs, gait analysis, torque assistance strategies, and human-centered testing protocols.

Autonomous Ice Cream Truck Object Follower Robot

Designed and built an educational robot using an Arduino Uno, Pixy2 color camera, L298N motor driver, DC motors, a 3D printed PLA chassis, LCD display, and battery powered electronics. The robot tracks colored objects autonomously and is intended as a K through 12 STEM engagement tool.
